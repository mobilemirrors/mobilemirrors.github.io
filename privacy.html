Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

Mobile Mirrors operates a mobile automotive side-view mirror repair and replacement service. This policy outlines how we handle personal data collected through our website forms.

Information Collected

We collect your name, phone number, vehicle details, and service location strictly to provide accurate service quotes, schedule technicians, and coordinate on-site repairs.

Mobile Messaging Terms & Compliance

By providing a mobile phone number via our quote request form, you consent to receive transactional text messages (such as repair quotes, scheduling confirmations, and arrival updates) from Mobile Mirrors.

No personal information, including mobile phone numbers or opt-in consent data, will be shared, rented, or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our number. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

For help, reply "HELP" to any message or contact our support team directly via our listed corporate email.
